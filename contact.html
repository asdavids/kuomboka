Zambia Tourism

Kuomboka & the Future of Cultural Tourism

Kuomboka represents one of Zambia's greatest cultural tourism assets. Since 1964, the ceremony has grown from a regional royal tradition into an internationally recognised event that draws visitors from across Africa, Europe, and beyond.


Revenue from tourism directly benefits the Lozi community — supporting accommodation providers, local guides, craft sellers, and transport operators in one of Zambia's least-developed provinces. By attending Kuomboka respectfully, visitors contribute to the preservation of a living cultural heritage.


We believe the future of Kuomboka tourism is one that puts the Lozi community first — where visitors come as guests, learn as students of culture, and leave as ambassadors of Lozi heritage.
