Zambia Tourism

Kuomboka & the Future of Cultural Tourism

Kuomboka represents one of Zambia's greatest cultural tourism assets. The ceremony has grown from a regional royal tradition into an internationally recognised event drawing visitors from across Africa, Europe, and beyond.


Revenue from tourism directly benefits the Lozi community — supporting accommodation providers, local guides, craft sellers, and transport operators in one of Zambia's most culturally rich provinces. By attending Kuomboka respectfully, visitors contribute to the preservation of a living cultural heritage.
